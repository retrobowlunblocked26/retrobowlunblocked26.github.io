🕹️ What is 12 Minibattles?

12 Minibattles is a local 2-player party game. The game randomly selects from a pool of 12 action mini-games, ranging from gunfights and sword duels to sports and physics puzzles. The core mechanic is that each player only uses a single button on the keyboard. First player to win a set number of rounds wins the overall match.

Pro Tips for Mastering 12 Minibattles

🧘 Don't Just Mash

In games like the Sniper Duel or Axe Throwing, spamming your button will get you killed. Wait for the perfect rotation angle before striking.

🔨 Know When to Mash

In the wrestling or tug-of-war mini-games, timing doesn't matter. You simply have to press your button faster than your opponent.

🧠 Learn the Physics

In the soccer and flying games, pressing the button changes your momentum. Learn how gravity affects your character to control them better.

👀 Watch the Screen

The game changes randomly. Always pay attention to the title card before the round starts so you know what mechanics are currently active.
