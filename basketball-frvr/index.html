Pro Tips for Mastering Basketball Frvr

🔥 Hunt for Swishes

Hitting the rim gives you 1 point. But scoring a clean "Swish" gives you 2. Hit three swishes in a row, and your multiplier catches on fire, awarding massive points.

📐 Watch the Arc

Don't just flick the mouse randomly. Draw a straight, deliberate line toward the basket. A higher arc is usually safer than a flat, fast throw.

🎯 Lead the Target

When the hoop starts moving, do not aim where it is. Aim where it is going to be by the time your ball reaches the apex of its arc.

🪙 Collect Coins

Occasionally, coins will appear hovering over the net. Try to adjust your shot to collect them so you can unlock cool new ball designs.
