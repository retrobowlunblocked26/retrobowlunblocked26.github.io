🍌 What is Monkey Mart?

Monkey Mart is an idle-management tycoon game. Players control a monkey running a grocery store. The gameplay involves moving the character to farm crops, carrying the items to stock empty shelves, and standing at the cash register to collect money. Profits are used to unlock new aisles, machines, and hire automated employees.

  • Time and resource management
  • Physical stacking mechanics
  • Business expansion and automation
  • Employee hiring and upgrading
  • Local progress saving

🎮 How to Play

The entire game is controlled simply by moving your character.

  • WASD / Arrows Move your monkey around the store
  • 🌾 Walk near plants to automatically harvest them
  • 🛒 Walk near empty shelves to automatically stock them
  • 💰 Stand in the cash register circle to process payments
  • ⬆️ Stand in green upgrade circles to spend money

Pro Tips for Mastering Monkey Mart

🎒 Upgrade Capacity

Your most important early upgrade is carrying capacity. Being able to carry 20 bananas at once saves you endless trips between the farm and the shelves.

👨‍🌾 Hire Cashiers First

Customers will get angry and leave if they wait too long at the register. Hire a cashier as soon as possible so you can focus entirely on farming and stocking.

💤 Wake Up Workers

Keep an eye on your hired employees. Sometimes they will fall asleep on the job. You have to walk over to them and bump into them to wake them up!

🏭 Focus on Machines

Processed goods (like puree or coffee) sell for much more money than raw ingredients. Always keep your processing machines fully loaded to maximize profits.

Complete Guide to Monkey Mart Unblocked

Monkey Mart is an incredibly charming and highly addictive business management and tycoon game. You play as an entrepreneurial monkey who has just opened a small supermarket. Your job is to handle literally everything: you must plant banana trees, harvest the crops, carry them to the display shelves, and then run to the cash register to check out your waiting monkey customers.

This unblocked version is perfect for players who love the satisfaction of building something from scratch. As you earn money, the game transforms from a fast-paced action game into a strategic management simulator. You will unlock new aisles, complex products like banana puree and coffee, and eventually hire a team of AI workers to automate the store for you.

Managing Your Retail Empire

  • The Core Loop: Harvest -> Stock -> Sell -> Upgrade. You must constantly run back and forth to keep the shelves full and the customers happy.
  • Unlock Upgrades: Stand in designated upgrade circles to spend your cash. You can increase your monkey's carrying capacity, movement speed, or expand the store.
  • Hire Employees: You cannot do it all alone. Hire cashiers to handle the money, stockers to refill shelves, and farmers to tend the crops.
  • Process Products: Later in the game, raw bananas aren't enough. You must buy machines that turn bananas into expensive goods like puree and chocolate.
