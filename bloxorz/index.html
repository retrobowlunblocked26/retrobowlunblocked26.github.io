📐 What is Bloxorz?

Bloxorz is a spatial logic puzzle game. The player must navigate a rectangular metal block across a floating platform to drop it through a square hole. The challenge comes from the block's dimensions (1x2); rolling it changes its orientation and footprint. Players must avoid falling off the edge, avoid shattering fragile tiles, and use switches to manipulate bridges.

  • 3D spatial movement puzzle
  • Grid-based movement mechanics
  • Weight-sensitive environmental switches
  • Block-splitting mechanics
  • 33 progressive difficulty levels

🎮 How to Play

The game uses the arrow keys to tumble the block across the grid.

  • ↑ / ↓ Roll the block Up or Down
  • ← / → Roll the block Left or Right
  • Spacebar Switch between halves (when the block is split)
  • 🛑 Do not let the block hang entirely off the edge
  • 🏆 Get the block to fall perfectly straight down the hole

Pro Tips for Mastering Bloxorz

📝 Save Your Passcode

At the top right of the screen during a stage, you will see a 6-digit passcode. Write this down! You can type it into the 'Load Stage' menu later to skip levels you've already beaten.

🧱 Watch the Orange Tiles

Orange tiles are fragile. You can lay the block flat across them safely because the weight is distributed. If you stand the block vertically on one, you will fall through.

🔄 Work Backwards

If a level seems impossible, look at the hole and trace the moves backward in your mind. Figure out exactly how the block needs to be positioned right before it falls in.

✖️ Heavy vs Light Switches

The circular switches trigger if any part of the block touches them. The X-shaped switches ONLY trigger if your block is standing completely vertical on them.

Complete Guide to Bloxorz Unblocked

Bloxorz is a legendary puzzle game that has been a staple in computer labs and schools for over a decade. The premise is elegant but incredibly challenging: you control a 1x2 rectangular metal block on a floating stage made of square tiles. Your goal is to roll the block so that it eventually stands completely vertical and drops into a 1x1 square hole at the end of the stage.

This unblocked version preserves all 33 original stages, brought back to life without the need for a Flash player. The game is a true test of spatial reasoning and geometric logic. Because the block is two squares long, it takes up different amounts of space depending on whether it is laying flat or standing up. You must plan your moves carefully so you don't roll off the edge of the map.

Navigating the Floating Stages

  • Switches and Bridges: You will encounter X and O switches. Landing on these will create or destroy bridges connecting different parts of the stage.
  • Heavy vs Soft Switches: 'O' switches are soft and can be activated no matter how the block lies on them. 'X' switches are heavy; the block must be standing perfectly vertical on top of them to trigger the mechanism.
  • Orange Tiles: Fragile orange tiles cannot support the full weight of the block. If you stand your block vertically on an orange tile, it will shatter and you will fall.
  • Teleporters: In later stages, stepping on a teleporter splits your block into two separate 1x1 cubes, requiring you to control them individually.
