🧬 What is Bitlife?

Bitlife is a life simulator and strategy game. The interface is entirely text-based. Players press an "Age Up" button to advance time year by year. Throughout their life, players are presented with multiple-choice scenarios. They must manage four main stats (Happiness, Health, Smarts, Looks) while navigating education, careers, relationships, and financial investments.

  • Text-based interactive fiction
  • Comprehensive economic system
  • Randomized life events
  • Multiple career and crime paths
  • Local progress saving

🎮 How to Play

The game relies entirely on navigating menus with your mouse or touch screen.
