🍪 What is Cookie Clicker?

Cookie Clicker is an incremental idle strategy game. You start by manually clicking a large cookie to earn currency. Managing Your Cookie Economy

  • Active Clicking: In the early game, your own mouse clicks are your main source of income. Click fast to get your first upgrades.
  • Passive Income (CpS): Buying buildings increases your "Cookies per Second". This means the game plays itself even when you aren't clicking.
  • Upgrades: Don't just buy buildings. Always invest in upgrades (the small icons on the right) as they can double the efficiency of your grandmas or factories.
  • Golden Cookies: Pay attention to the screen. Occasionally, a Golden Cookie will appear. Clicking it gives you a massive, temporary boost to your production.
