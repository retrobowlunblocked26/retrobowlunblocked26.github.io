Pro Tips for Mastering Age Of War

⭐ Evolve Quickly

Prioritize saving XP to evolve rather than spamming special attacks. A higher-tier era easily destroys units from a lower era.

🛡️ Turrets are Key

If you are losing ground, stop spawning units and build a strong turret. Let the enemy come to your base where your turret can kill them for free gold.

⚔️ Mix Your Units

Never spam just one unit type. Always send a cheap, high-health melee unit in front to absorb damage while your ranged units shoot from behind.

⏳ Save the Special

Your special attack takes a long time to recharge. Only use it if a massive wave of enemies is about to hit your base.
