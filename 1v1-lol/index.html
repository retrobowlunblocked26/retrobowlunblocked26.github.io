Pro Tips for Mastering 1v1 LOL

⚙️ Change Your Binds

The default building keys (Z, X, C, V) are difficult for many players to reach quickly. Go to settings and change them to keys near WASD, like Q, E, F, and Shift.

🧱 Box Up to Heal

If you take heavy damage, don't run away in the open. Build four walls around yourself and a roof (a 'box') to safely reload and reassess the situation.

🔫 The Shotgun Swap

The pump shotgun deals massive damage but shoots slowly. Fire a shotgun blast, then immediately swap to your Assault Rifle to finish the enemy off.

👀 Use Third Person

You can see around corners without exposing yourself. Hide behind a wall you built, rotate your camera to see the enemy, and edit a window to shoot them.
