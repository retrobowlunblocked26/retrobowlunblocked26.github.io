Pro Tips for Mastering Basket Swooshes

⚡ Speed is Crucial

Do not spend 5 seconds lining up the perfect shot. The AI shoots fast. A "good enough" fast shot is better than a perfect slow shot.

🎯 High Arc

Aiming high creates a steeper drop into the net, reducing the chance of the ball bouncing off the rim and out.

🔄 Adjust Quickly

If you miss, don't completely redraw your line. Keep your mouse in the same general area and just make a micro-adjustment for the second shot.

🛑 Ignore the Opponent

Seeing the opponent's ball fly through the air can be distracting. Focus only on your own aiming line.
