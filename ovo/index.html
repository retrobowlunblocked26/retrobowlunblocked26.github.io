Pro Tips for Mastering OvO

🚀 Master the Slide-Jump

The standard jump is too weak for most gaps. You must get used to pressing DOWN to slide, and immediately pressing UP to perform a massive long jump.

🦅 Dive Recovery

If you jump too far and are about to hit spikes, you can quickly press DOWN to dive straight down to the ground, saving your life.

🧱 Wall Clinging

You slide down walls slower than you fall. Use walls to pause, assess the spikes below you, and time your wall-jump perfectly.

💀 Embrace Failure

The levels are short and respawns are instant. Don't play cautiously; push your speed to the limit to learn the muscle memory required.
