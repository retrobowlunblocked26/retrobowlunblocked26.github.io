🍮 What is Jelly Truck?

Jelly Truck is a 2D physics racing and puzzle game. You control a vehicle that behaves like a gelatinous solid. Instead of crashing and taking damage, the truck squishes, bends, and stretches. The player must use this unique soft-body physics mechanic to climb over bizarre obstacles, rotating gears, and moving platforms to reach the end of the stage.

Pro Tips for Mastering Jelly Truck

🚗 Squeeze Through

Don't be afraid of tight spaces. Drive hard into small gaps, and your truck will naturally squish down and pop out the other side.

🛑 Don't Rush

Because the truck is so bouncy, holding the gas constantly will cause you to flip uncontrollably. Drive slowly over complicated rotating gears.

🦅 Air Control

When you are launched into the air, use the lean buttons to position your wheels downward. The jelly tires act as massive shock absorbers.

🔙 Rocking the Boat

If you get stuck in a dip, quickly alternate between driving forward and backward to build a rocking momentum that will eventually launch you out.

Complete Guide to Jelly Truck Unblocked

Jelly Truck is one of the most uniquely entertaining physics driving games on the internet. In this weird and wonderful world, everything—from the obstacles to the ground, and even your own truck—is made out of squishy, deformable jelly. Your objective is to drive this wobbly vehicle from the start of the level to the finish line without getting hopelessly stuck or wedged in a gap.

Because your truck is soft, traditional driving rules don't apply. You can squeeze through spaces that seem too small, bounce over giant gaps, and even drive entirely upside down if you get enough momentum. This unblocked version lets you experience all the squishy fun directly in your browser.

Mastering the Jelly Physics

  • Deformation is Key: Use the softness of your truck to your advantage. If you hit a wall, push the gas to squish your truck down, allowing your front wheels to grip the top of the obstacle.
  • Momentum Swings: Because the truck wobbles, braking suddenly will throw the cabin forward. You can use this rocking motion to vault over gaps.
  • Lean Control: Just like a dirt bike game, you must use the left and right arrows to lean your truck. Keeping your wheels on the ground is the only way to accelerate.
  • Transformations: In later levels, driving through special gates will transform your truck into different jelly shapes, each with unique handling properties.
