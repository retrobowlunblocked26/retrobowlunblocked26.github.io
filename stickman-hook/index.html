Pro Tips for Mastering Stickman Hook

⚖️ Trust the Bounce

In later levels, the anchor points are spread far apart. Trust the bouncy floors to carry your momentum instead of trying to hook onto everything.

⏱️ Release Early

If you hold the rope too long, you will swing backwards. Release your hook right before you reach the apex of your swing to get maximum forward distance.

🔄 Spam the Hook

If you are falling and about to die, rapidly spam the click button. Sometimes you will catch an off-screen anchor point at the last possible millisecond.

🧘 Find the Flow

Don't fight the physics. Let your stickman fall to build gravity, then use that speed to slingshot yourself across the map.
