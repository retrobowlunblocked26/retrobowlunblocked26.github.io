🍄 What is Super Mario Bros?

Super Mario Bros is a 2D side-scrolling platformer. The player must navigate through 32 levels across 8 worlds, avoiding hazards, jumping on enemies, and collecting coins. Touching an enemy shrinks or kills Mario, but finding a Super Mushroom makes him larger and grants an extra hit point. The ultimate goal is to defeat Bowser in his castle.

  • Classic side-scrolling platforming
  • Momentum-based jumping physics
  • Hidden secrets and warp zones
  • Iconic enemy types (Koopas, Goombas)
  • Original 8-bit graphics and audio

Pro Tips for Mastering Super Mario Bros

🏃 Always Sprint

To get the maximum jump height and distance, you must be holding the Sprint button before you jump. It is essential for late-game gaps.

🐢 Kick the Shells

Jumping on a Koopa Troopa turns it into a shell. Kick it to send it sliding forward, destroying any enemies in its path—but jump over it if it bounces back!

⭐ Find the Starman

Certain blocks contain a flashing Star. Touching it grants you temporary invincibility, allowing you to run straight through enemies for massive points.

🚩 Hit the Flagpole

At the end of a level, aim for the very top of the flagpole. The higher you grab it, the more points you earn, up to a maximum of 5,000.
