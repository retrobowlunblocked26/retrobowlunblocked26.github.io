Pro Tips for Mastering 2 Minute Football

🏃 Don't Panic in the Pocket

When the defense breaks through, don't just throw the ball randomly. Keep moving backward and sideways to buy an extra second for your receiver to get open.

🎯 Lead the Target

The ball takes time to travel through the air. You must click slightly ahead of your running receiver so the ball meets them in the future.

🛑 Stop the Clock

If time is running out, aim your passes toward the sidelines. If your receiver steps out of bounds after catching the ball, the timer pauses.

🛡️ Take the Sack

Sometimes nobody is open. It is better to just take the tackle (sack) and lose a few yards than to throw a risky pass that gets intercepted.
