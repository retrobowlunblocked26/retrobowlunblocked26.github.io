Mastering the One-Button Drift

  • The Core Mechanic: The car drives straight and slightly to the left automatically. When you press and hold the button, the car drifts hard to the right.
  • Timing is Everything: If you press the button too early, you turn into the inside corner. If you release it too late, you fly off the outside edge.
  • Collect Coins: Coins are scattered along the dangerous edges of the track. Risking your run to collect them allows you to unlock faster, better-handling vehicles like police cars and muscle cars.
  • Use Boosters: Before a run begins, you can spend coins on boosters like Coin Rush (double coins) or Auto-Insurance (saves you from falling once).
