2. Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

To file a copyright infringement notification, you must provide a written communication that includes substantially the following:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work: Describe the work claimed to have been infringed, or if multiple works, a representative list.
  • Identification of infringing material: Provide URLs or specific locations on our site where the infringing material appears.
  • Your contact information: Include your name, address, telephone number, and email address.
  • Statement of good faith: "I have a good faith belief that use of the copyrighted material described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law."
  • Statement of accuracy: "The information in this notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, I am the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed."
  • Signature: Physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative.
