Pro Tips for Mastering Moto X3M

🔄 Flip Constantly

To get 3 stars on later levels, you must do flips. If you have enough air time, always try to squeeze in a backflip to reduce your timer.

🛑 Use the Brake

When approaching a steep downhill, tapping the brake allows your front wheel to drop safely onto the ramp instead of flying over it into a wall.

🦅 Land Parallel

Always adjust your rotation in the air so your wheels are perfectly parallel to the ground you are landing on. This prevents you from bouncing out of control.

🔥 Memorize the Track

The traps move fast. Treat your first run as a practice to learn where the bombs and spikes are, then do a serious run for the star rating.
