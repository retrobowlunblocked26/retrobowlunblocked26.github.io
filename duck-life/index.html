Training Your Champion Duck

  • Running: Dodge falling objects by moving left and right. The longer you survive, the higher the skill level goes.
  • Flying: Click and hold to fly up, release to drop. Navigate through gaps in the terrain to earn flying points.
  • Swimming: Jump over obstacles in the water or dive under them. Timing is everything.
  • Feeding: Training depletes energy. Always buy regular or skill-boosting seeds from the shop to keep your duck at maximum stamina before a race.
