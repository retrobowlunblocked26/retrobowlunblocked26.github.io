Is Retro Bowl unblocked at school?

Yes! Our version is optimized to bypass strict school firewalls and network restrictions. It runs perfectly on school-issued Chromebooks without requiring any administrator privileges.

Will my franchise progress be saved?

Absolutely. The game uses your browser's local storage (cache) to save your seasons automatically. As long as you don't clear your browser data, you can pick up exactly where you left off.

Do I play defense in Retro Bowl?

No, you only control the offense and the kicking game. Defense is simulated based on your Defensive Coordinator's rating and the quality of your defensive star players.

How do I throw a bullet pass?

To throw a faster, lower-trajectory 'bullet pass', drag to aim your throw as usual, then tap the screen (or click with another finger/mouse button) before releasing. This is great for tight windows.
