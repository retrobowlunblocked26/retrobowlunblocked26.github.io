Pro Tips for Mastering Ape Sling

⚡ Don't Wait

The screen is always scrolling. If you spend too much time aiming the perfect shot, you will fall off the bottom of the screen and die.

📏 Micro Slings

You don't always need maximum power. Often, a tiny, weak pull is the best way to get over a small obstacle without flying out of control.

🎯 Ricochet Shots

Sometimes a direct path is blocked by a saw. Aim hard at the side wall to bounce around the trap and hit your target safely.

💰 Risk vs Reward

Coins are often placed near dangerous traps. If you are going for a high score, ignore the coins and focus purely on survival.

Ape Sling is an incredibly addictive, fast-paced arcade skill game. You control a bouncy ape character moving up an endless vertical tunnel. Instead of running or jumping, your only method of movement is a slingshot mechanic. You click, pull back on the ape to build tension, aim, and release to launch him to the next grab-point.

Mastering the Sling Mechanic

  • Trajectory is Everything: When you pull back, a dotted line appears. Use this to thread the needle between tight traps.
  • Bounce off Walls: Your ape is bouncy. You can deliberately shoot into a wall to ricochet off it and reach a difficult grab-point.
  • Momentum: If you are moving fast, you don't always need to stop and aim. You can "quick-sling" while still in the air to maintain incredible speed.
  • Collect Coins: Grab floating coins to unlock cool new cosmetic skins and characters in the shop.
