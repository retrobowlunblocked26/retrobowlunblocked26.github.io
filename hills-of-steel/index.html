🚜 What is Hills Of Steel?

Hills Of Steel is a 2D physics-based action game. Players drive a tank over uneven, hilly terrain while engaging in combat with enemy forces. The core mechanic requires the player to use the slopes of the environment to angle their tank, which in turn aims their weapon. Earning coins allows players to upgrade their tank's stats or buy entirely new vehicles.

Pro Tips for Mastering Hills Of Steel

🛡️ Reverse to Survive

If a massive boss tank approaches, don't drive toward it. Constantly drive in reverse while firing. This keeps you out of their range while your lobbed shells deal damage.

💥 Beware Splash Damage

Shooting an enemy when they are right next to you will cause explosion damage to your own tank. Back up before you fire!

🚁 Focus the Helicopters

Helicopters are incredibly annoying and will chip away at your health from above. Always prioritize finding a steep hill to angle your gun up and destroy them first.

💰 Upgrade the Gun First

A good offense is the best defense. Upgrading your gun damage ensures you can one-shot weaker enemies before they have a chance to fire back.

Complete Guide to Hills Of Steel Unblocked

Hills Of Steel combines the addictive vehicle mechanics of hill-climbing games with the explosive artillery combat of tank shooters. You command a heavy battle tank rolling across rugged, uneven terrain. Your objective is to survive endless waves of enemy tanks, jeeps, and attack helicopters. Because the game relies entirely on physics, you cannot simply point your mouse and shoot.

In this unblocked version, the angle of your tank's chassis determines the trajectory of your cannon. You must use the slopes of the hills to aim your barrel up or down. It creates a highly strategic action game where positioning is just as important as firepower.

Mastering Tank Combat

  • Use the Terrain: If an enemy is far away, drive the front of your tank up a hill to angle your cannon upwards and lob shells over the terrain.
  • Ramming Damage: If you get too close, shooting might cause splash damage to yourself. Sometimes it is better to hit the gas and physically crush a weaker vehicle under your tank treads.
  • Upgrade Your Arsenal: Collect coins from destroyed enemies to upgrade your tank's armor, engine power, and gun damage.
  • Unlock New Tanks: As you progress, you can unlock devastating new vehicles like the Titan (heavy armor), the Joker (fast flips), or the futuristic Reaper.
