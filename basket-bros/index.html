🏀 What is Basket Bros?

Basket Bros is a 2D arcade sports game featuring 1v1 basketball matches. It ignores traditional rules, allowing players to physically punch each other to steal the ball. The game focuses on exaggerated physics, massive jumps, and epic dunks. You can play a tournament against AI or play locally against a friend.

🎮 How to Play

Controls for Player 1 (Player 2 uses Arrows and L).

  • WASD Move and Jump
  • Space / G Shoot the ball
  • Space / G Punch (when you don't have the ball)
  • 🗑️ Jump near the hoop to trigger an automatic dunk
  • 🛡️ Defend your hoop by punching the attacker

Pro Tips for Mastering Basket Bros

🥊 Aggressive Defense

Do not wait for them to shoot. Run directly at the opponent and spam the punch button to knock them down before they cross half-court.

🏀 Easy Dunks

Dunking is almost unstoppable. If you are close to the basket, don't tap shoot—just jump towards the rim and the game will do the rest.

🎯 Time Your Shots

When shooting from far away, jump first, then release the shoot button at the very top of your jump to get maximum distance and arc.

👕 Know Your Bro

Pay attention to the stats of the character you unlock. If you pick a slow, heavy character, don't try to shoot three-pointers—stick to dunking.
