🐒 What is Temple Run 2?

Temple Run 2 is an iconic 3D endless runner. The player's character sprints automatically while being chased by a giant monkey. Pro Tips for Mastering Temple Run 2

👀 Look at the Horizon

As the game speeds up, looking at your character will get you killed. Look far ahead down the path so your brain can process the sequence of jumps and turns before you reach them.

🛡️ Upgrade the Shield

Spend your first batch of coins in the store to upgrade the Shield duration. It acts as a free extra life if you accidentally hit a fire trap or tree root.

🎢 Pre-Turn in the Air

If a sharp turn comes immediately after a jump, you don't have to wait to land. You can press the turn button while still in mid-air to queue the action.

💎 Save Your Gems

Green gems can be used to resurrect yourself after dying. Save them for when you are on a massive, record-breaking run. Don't waste them early in the game.

Surviving the Escape

  • Navigation: The game moves fast. You must swipe left or right to take sharp 90-degree turns. Missing a turn means falling into the abyss.
  • Obstacles: Swipe up to jump over tree roots and fire, and swipe down to slide under low-hanging rocks and ruins.
  • The Minecart: In the mineshaft sections, you don't run. You must lean your cart left or right to balance on single rails and avoid smashing into dead ends.
  • Power-Ups: Collect glowing green gems and coins to activate a shield, a coin magnet, or a massive speed boost that makes you temporarily invincible.
