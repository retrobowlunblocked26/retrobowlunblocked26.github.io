Pro Tips for Mastering Masked Forces

🎯 Burst Fire

Holding down the trigger on an assault rifle creates massive recoil, causing your bullets to fly everywhere. Fire in short 2-3 round bursts to maintain perfect accuracy.

🏃 Strafe During Combat

Never stand still while shooting. Constantly tap 'A' and 'D' to move side-to-side (strafing). This makes it much harder for the AI enemies to hit you.

🔪 The Knife is Faster

If you run completely out of ammo in a close-quarters fight, it is often faster to switch to your knife and rush the enemy than it is to wait through a long reload animation.

💰 Save for the Sniper

The early SMGs are decent, but a good Sniper Rifle allows you to complete missions safely from across the map. Save your cash for a high-damage rifle.

Mastering the Firefight

  • Aim for the Head: Headshots deal critical damage and reward you with bonus cash at the end of the round. Take the extra half-second to line up your crosshairs.
  • Use Cover: The AI enemies are accurate. Do not stand in the open. Use walls, crates, and buildings to break their line of sight, pop out to shoot, and reload in safety.
  • Manage Your Ammo: You don't have unlimited bullets. Make your shots count, and always switch to your sidearm if your primary weapon runs empty during a close-quarters fight.
  • Upgrade Smartly: Before buying the most expensive sniper rifle, invest in basic body armor to increase your survivability in the early missions.
