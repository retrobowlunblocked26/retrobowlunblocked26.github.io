🐱 What is Cat Trap?

Cat Trap is a turn-based puzzle game played on a hexagonal grid. The objective is to prevent a smart AI cat from reaching the edge of the board. You make a move by clicking a light hexagon to turn it dark (impassable). After your move, the cat moves one space toward the edge. You win if the cat is completely surrounded by dark hexes.

Complete Guide to Cat Trap Unblocked

Cat Trap (also known as Catch the Cat) is a deceptively simple, turn-based logic puzzle that will seriously test your strategic foresight. The game takes place on a grid of light green hexagons with a black cat sitting right in the middle. Your goal is to trap the cat so it cannot reach the edge of the board. Every time you click a hexagon to turn it into an impassable dark wall, the cat takes one step toward freedom.

Our unblocked version is a favorite among students who enjoy mental challenges. The AI controlling the cat is incredibly smart; it always calculates the shortest path to the edge. If you just randomly click near the cat, you will lose every time. You have to think several moves ahead to build a wide perimeter and slowly close the net.

The Art of Trapping

  • Don't play too close: The biggest mistake beginners make is clicking the hex right next to the cat. The cat will just go around it.
  • Build a wide circle: Start building your walls far away from the cat, near the edges of the board.
  • Connect the dots: The board starts with a few random dark hexes already filled in. Use these to your advantage by connecting your walls to them.
  • Look for the shortest path: Always look at where the cat is heading and block that path from a distance.
