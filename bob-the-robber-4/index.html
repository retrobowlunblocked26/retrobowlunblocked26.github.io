Pro Tips for Mastering Bob The Robber 4

👀 Watch the Patrols

Never rush into a new room. Stand in the doorway and observe the guard's walking pattern before making your move.

🌑 Shadows are Safe

If a camera or guard is approaching, find a dark spot on the wall and stop moving. They will walk right past you.

📝 Read Everything

Don't guess passwords. Look for sticky notes on walls or in filing cabinets—they always contain the code for the nearest laser grid.

🛒 Buy Gadgets

Use the money you steal to buy items like the Stun Gun or Skeleton Key before starting a hard level. They are lifesavers.
