🦊 What is Animal Arena?

Animal Arena is a local party brawler. Up to four players control animals that constantly run forward. By using a single button, players jump and double-jump to change direction, avoid traps, and stomp on their friends' heads. The last surviving animal wins the round.

  • Local 4-player shared-screen

🎮 How to Play

Each player is assigned one specific key on the keyboard.

  • Action Key Press once to jump
  • Action Key (Mid-air) Press again to double-jump and change direction
  • 🎯 Land on another player's head to eliminate them

🦅 Take the High Ground
