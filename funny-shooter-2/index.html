Pro Tips for Mastering Funny Shooter 2

🏃 Kiting Strategy

Never stand still. The most effective strategy is to walk backward in a large circle around the map, grouping all the enemies into a tight pack to shoot them.

💣 Explosive Value

Save up for the RPG or Grenade Launcher early. Because enemies swarm you in large groups, area-of-effect (splash) damage is incredibly powerful.

🎯 Use ADS

Holding Right-Click zooms in and significantly reduces weapon spread. Use this to score easy headshots on approaching giants.

🪙 Collect the Coins

Coins disappear after a short while. Make sure you run over the areas where you killed enemies to collect your money before the next wave starts.
