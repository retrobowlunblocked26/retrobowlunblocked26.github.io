🧩 What is Stick Merge?

Stick Merge is a hybrid puzzle/action game. In the puzzle phase, players manage a grid of weapons. By dragging and dropping two identical weapons together, they merge into a single, more powerful weapon. In the action phase, players take their highest-level weapon into a first-person shooting gallery to defeat stickman targets and earn money to buy more weapons.

  • Drag-and-drop merging mechanics
  • Inventory grid management
  • Reflex-based shooting gallery
  • Extensive weapon tech tree
  • Incremental economic progression

🎮 How to Play

The game uses the mouse for both puzzle solving and combat.

  • 🖱️ Click & Drag (Armory) Drag a weapon onto an identical one to merge them
  • 🖱️ Click & Drag (Combat) Drag your mouse to move the crosshair
  • 🔫 The gun fires automatically when hovering over an enemy
  • 💀 Shoot enemies quickly before they deplete your health bar
  • 🛒 Click the shop icon to buy weapons with cash

Pro Tips for Mastering Stick Merge

🧹 Keep the Board Clear

Boxes will stop dropping if your grid is full. Always prioritize merging your lowest-level weapons first to create empty space for free drops.

🎯 Pre-Aim

During the shooting phase, enemies pop out from the same cover spots. Keep your crosshair near the top of the boxes to instantly get headshots when they appear.

🛡️ Don't Rush the Boss

If you are failing the combat phase, go back to the armory and spend some time merging. Entering combat with a weapon just one tier higher makes a massive difference.

📦 Shoot the Loot

Never ignore the wooden supply crates that pop up during firefights. They often drop weapons that instantly appear on your merge board.

Complete Guide to Stick Merge Unblocked

Stick Merge brilliantly combines two highly addictive genres: idle merge-puzzles and reflex-based shooting galleries. The game is split into two distinct phases. In the armory, you use strategy to combine identical weapons—merge two basic pistols to create a submachine gun, merge two SMGs to create an assault rifle, and so on. Once your arsenal is ready, you take your best gun out into the alley to shoot down waves of enemy stickmen.

This unblocked version provides the perfect gameplay loop for school breaks. The merging phase is relaxing and strategic, requiring you to manage your inventory space. The shooting phase is fast-paced and action-packed, rewarding you with cash to buy more weapons to merge.

Mastering the Gameplay Loop

  • Always Be Merging: Keep your armory board clean. Always drag matching weapons together to free up space so new weapon crates can drop.
  • Buy Smartly: You can buy base-level weapons with your cash. Sometimes it's better to buy a mid-tier weapon directly rather than building it from scratch.
  • Aim for the Head: During the shooting phase, dragging your crosshair over an enemy's head deals massive damage and saves your limited health.
  • Watch for Crates: While shooting, wooden crates will occasionally appear. Shoot them immediately to get cash bonuses and weapon drops.
