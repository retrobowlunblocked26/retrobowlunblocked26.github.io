🐰 What is Poor Bunny?

Poor Bunny is an endless score-attack survival game. Set in a closed arena, the player must collect carrots to increase their score. With each carrot collected, new hazards spawn, filling the screen with projectiles and traps. The game ends when the player takes a single hit. It features a massive roster of unlockable characters.

  • Single-screen arcade action
  • Progressive difficulty scaling
  • One-hit kill mechanics
  • Local cooperative multiplayer
  • Visual hazard warnings

Pro Tips for Mastering Poor Bunny

⬇️ Fast Falling

If you are in the air and a laser targets you, press the DOWN key to fast-fall to the ground and dodge the attack.

🚀 Bait the Missiles

Homing missiles will follow you. Run toward a wall and jump over them at the last second so they crash into the wall and explode safely.

🤝 Co-op Strategy

In 2-player mode, split the arena. Have one player stay on the left and one on the right. This prevents you from jumping into each other and makes collecting carrots much faster.

🧘 Stay Centered

When waiting for the next carrot to spawn, try to stay near the center of the map. This gives you the shortest possible distance to travel no matter where it appears.

Complete Guide to Poor Bunny Unblocked

Poor Bunny is a high-octane arcade survival game that combines adorable pixel art with brutally fast gameplay. You control a small bunny in a single-screen arena. Your only objective is to collect carrots that spawn randomly. However, every time you grab a carrot, a new, deadly trap is added to the arena—lasers, homing missiles, falling spikes, and buzzsaws.

This unblocked version is incredible for quick reflex training. It supports both solo play, where you try to beat your personal high score, and a fantastic local co-op mode. In co-op, you and a friend share the same keyboard to collect carrots together, bringing teamwork (and chaotic panic) to the arena.

Surviving the Arena

  • Constant Movement: Standing still is a death sentence. You must constantly run and jump to avoid the crossfire of lasers and bouncy spikes.
  • Watch the Warnings: Before a laser fires or a spike falls, the game gives you a brief visual warning (a red line or exclamation mark). Use this split-second to move out of the way.
  • Collect Carrots Quickly: The longer you take to collect a carrot, the more time the traps have to hit you. Be aggressive with your pathing.
  • Unlock the Roster: The carrots you collect act as currency. Use them in the main menu to unlock over 100 different cute bunny skins.
